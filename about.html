About Gulf South Automation

Gulf South Automation is a New Orleans-based business automation company built specifically for local service businesses. We exist because small businesses — plumbers, HVAC techs, electricians, landscapers, and every trade in between — deserve the same tools that big companies use to capture and keep customers.

The reality is simple: when you're on a job, you can't always answer the phone. When a customer doesn't hear back, they move on. We built GSA to solve that problem with smart, affordable automation that works for you around the clock.

"When your follow-ups, review requests, and lead capture run automatically, you get something back that's hard to put a price on — time. Time to focus on the job in front of you, take on more work, grow your business, or simply get home at the end of the day without a pile of callbacks waiting."

Why Gulf South Automation

We're not a national software company. We're local, we understand the Greater New Orleans market, and we build solutions that fit the way you actually do business — whether that's a single automation or a fully custom system built around your specific needs.

Every business is different. That's why we don't believe in one-size-fits-all packages. We take the time to understand your workflow and build something that actually works for the way you operate.
