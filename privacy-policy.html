Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Gulf South Automation ("we", "us", or "our") operates as a business automation service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. Gulf South Automation is owned and operated by Timothy A. Cronin Jr., a Louisiana-based automation service provider.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and business name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Business information when you contact us or submit a web form

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide our services
  • Send transactional and informational SMS communications related to your inquiry or service
  • Improve and maintain our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

SMS Communications

By contacting us or submitting a web form, you consent to receive SMS messages from Gulf South Automation regarding your inquiry. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. For help, reply HELP.

We use your phone number solely to send automated SMS responses to your inquiries and to provide the automation services you have requested. SMS opt-in information is never shared with third parties.

Message frequency will vary based on your interactions with our service. Standard messaging rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@gulfsouthautomation.com.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party service providers to help operate our business, including SMS delivery services. These providers only receive the information necessary to perform their specific functions and are not permitted to use your information for any other purpose.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
